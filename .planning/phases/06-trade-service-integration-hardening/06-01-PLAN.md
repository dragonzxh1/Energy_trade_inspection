<objective>
Close GAP-2 (ARCH-02): surface circuit breaker degradation in the trade check path.

The OpenSanctions circuit breaker already returns `{ status: 'ok' | 'degraded', ... }` from `checkSanctions()`, but `trade-service.ts` never reads the `status` field — the `.catch()` fallback also discards it. This plan adds `sanctionDegraded?: boolean` to `TradeCheckResult`, reads `status` from both sanction calls, and renders an amber informational warning box in `TradeClient.tsx` when the field is true.

Purpose: Compliance officers need to know when sanction data may be stale. A silent degraded-circuit result is a compliance liability.
Output: Updated `trade-service.ts` with status propagation + updated `TradeClient.tsx` with amber warning box.
</objective>
